ROAD TO PLATINUM

Where Music Knowledge Takes Center Stage.

🎯 Objective

Be the first player to reach Platinum by correctly identifying both the song title and artist before your opponents. Then complete the Platinum Encore to win!

🎲 Game Setup

  • Separate the cards into their genre piles.
  • Shuffle each pile.
  • Give every player one Remix Card.
  • Give every player one Double Time Card.
  • Use one phone or tablet to scan all QR codes.
  • The oldest player goes first.

🎵 Genre Colors

  • 🟨 Yellow — Country
  • 🟦 Blue — Pop
  • 🟪 Purple — Hip-Hop / Rap
  • 🟥 Red — Rock
  • 🟩 Green — One Hit Wonders
  • 🎨 Striped Die Face — Player's Choice

🎤 How to Play

  1. Roll the genre die.
  2. If you want to use your Double Time card, play it before the QR code is scanned.
  3. The designated player scans the top card from the matching genre pile.
  4. You have 20 seconds.
  5. You get ONE official guess.
  6. You must correctly name BOTH:
    • Song Title
    • Artist
  7. Correct Answer: Move to the next space matching the color rolled.
  8. Incorrect Answer: Remain where you are.

⭐ Remix Card

Play after another player answers incorrectly. You receive one chance to answer the same song. If correct, move to the next matching color. Discard after use. If multiple players want to Remix, priority goes clockwise.

⚡ Double Time Card

Play after rolling but before the QR code is scanned. If you answer correctly, move to the SECOND matching color instead of the first. Discard after use.

🏆 Reaching Platinum

When no spaces of your rolled color remain ahead of you, move directly into Platinum after answering correctly.

🎸 Platinum Encore

Once you reach Platinum, you must survive one final challenge. On your next turn:

  • Roll the die.
  • Scan a card.
  • Correctly identify BOTH the title and artist.
  • If correct—you WIN!
  • If incorrect—remain in Platinum and try again next turn.

👑 Platinum Showdown

If multiple players reach Platinum, everyone currently in Platinum may answer simultaneously. The first correct answer wins the game. If nobody answers correctly, play continues until another Platinum Showdown occurs.

🎬 Backstage Rules

  • One phone scans all QR codes.
  • No searching for answers.
  • One official guess.
  • 20 second time limit.
  • One Remix Card per player.
  • One Double Time Card per player.
